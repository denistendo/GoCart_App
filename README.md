# GoCart

GoCart is a Flutter-based mobile shopping platform that offers users a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience. Browse products, add them to your cart, and checkout with ease. Designed to be fast, secure, and user-friendly.

## Features

- User authentication (Email, Google, or social accounts)
- Browse products by category
- Product details with ratings and reviews
- Add to cart and wishlist functionality
- Checkout with multiple payment options
- Order history and tracking
- Profile and account management
- Push notifications for offers and order updates

## Screens

1. **Splash Screen** – Displays logo and app intro.  
2. **Onboarding Screens** – Highlights key features.  
3. **Login / Signup** – User authentication.  
4. **Home / Dashboard** – Browse featured products and categories.  
5. **Product Listing / Category** – View products by category.  
6. **Product Detail** – Product info, images, and reviews.  
7. **Cart** – Manage selected products.  
8. **Checkout** – Complete orders with shipping and payment options.  
9. **Order Confirmation** – Displays order success and details.  
10. **Profile / Account** – User info, order history, wishlist, settings.  
11. **Settings** – Manage notifications, language, and account options.  
12. **Search (Optional)** – Quickly find products.  
13. **Notifications (Optional)** – Receive updates and offers.
